FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Our kitchen can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergy-specific requirements. Please inform us when making your reservation so we can prepare accordingly.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. For parties of 6 or more, we require 48 hours notice. Late cancellations may incur a fee of $50 per person.

Is there parking available?

Complimentary valet parking is available during dinner service. There is also a public parking garage located one block east of our restaurant.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, digital and physical gift cards are available in any denomination. They can be purchased in-restaurant or by contacting us via email. The perfect gift for any occasion.
